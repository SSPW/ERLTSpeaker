Message Formatting
------------------

Text like this {n} will be replaced be the ERLT values listed below

 1. position
 2. pilot_name
 3. pilot_transponder_token
 4. pilot_quad
 5. pilot_team
 6. last_lap_num
 7. last_lap_time
 8. fastest_lap_num
 9. fastest_lap_time
 10. avg_lap_time
 11. best_time_phrase or nothing

**Examples**

lap {6}, time {7}, {11}

{2}, place {1}, lap {6}, time {7}, {11}
